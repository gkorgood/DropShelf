Who We Are

We are a group of Mechanical Engineering students at Manhattan's prestigious Cooper Union, and we are working to make small space living more manageable.

Next

What We Did

Living in small apartments is a challenge many people face daily, and going to school in New York City, it is not something we are strangers to. We want to help people alleviate crowded storage on their living room floors, and start getting people to use the expansive vertical space that as of yet doesn't get used efficiently.

Next

How We Did It

We devised a system in which shelves could be placed high on the wall, where they would normally be out of reach, sight, and mind. When the user needs something from the shelf, it can be pulled down effortlessly to eye level. Once the user is done with the shelf, it can be pushed back up to its original position, once again out of the way.
